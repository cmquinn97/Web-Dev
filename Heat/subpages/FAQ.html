Why do you like the Miami Heat?

No one in my family is a basketball fan, so I didn't inherit a favorite team , but basketball was awesome to me at a young age. When the Heat won the championship in 2006, they became my favorite team. It is embarrassing to admit that I became a fan because they won, but I have stuck with them since.

Who is your current favorite Heat player?

It is close between Justise Winslow and Hassan Whiteside. They both play hard. But Winslow has the awesome hair so I will pick him

Who is your all time favorite Heat player?

This is a tough one. On one hand their is Dwyane Wade, the face of the Miami Heat, and basically made this franchise. Then their is LeBron James, who was only hear for 4 years but took the Heat to finals 4 years in a row. When he joined the Heat I went from fan to die hard fan. It is really hard for me to pick between the 2.

What is your favorite Heat memory?

Ray Allen, game 6.
