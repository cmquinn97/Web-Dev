Team History

The Miami Heat is an American professional basketball team based in Miami, launched in 1988.In 2003, the Miami Heat franchise found its future in drafting Dwyane Wade. Wade led the heat to their first NBA championship in 2006. After a few down years, the Heat made possibly the biggest free agent signing in sports history, signing LeBron James in 2010. They went to 4 straight finals after acquiring James, winning 2 of them. The team has a total of 3 NBA championships.
